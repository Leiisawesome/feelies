## §1. Goal — pre-registered, with X derived rather than wished

- **"Winning alpha" is defined once:** STATUS `accepted` under the R10 template — full frozen pipeline through step 8 (post-latency, post-fill-model, post-cost-stack, inv12-stress SURVIVES, sensitivity-robust), RESEARCH→PAPER gate-ready on recorded evidence. Nothing less counts; no mid-campaign redefinition.
- **Primary goal: 1 winning alpha deployable across ≥ 5 symbols.** The cross-sectional grammar makes multi-symbol native — one Layer-3 alpha ranking a cohort *is* "X symbols" — so symbol-count is achieved through claim shape, not through repeating single-name campaigns.
- **Stretch: 2 winning alphas** (the second from a structurally distinct grammar branch — spread-state DV or a reopened single-name cell — never a mutation sibling of the first).
- **Derivation of the numbers:** budget affords ≤ 4 step-2 outcome contacts (§3). Round-1 base rate at 2b was 0/2 under the old grammar/universe; the thesis claims the two changed vertices lift the per-contact pass odds materially. 1 win in ≤ 4 contacts is the honest ambition consistent with taking the thesis seriously without pretending the base rate away. Lei may amend X at approval; the derivation logic freezes with the charter.

**Campaign stop-rule (frozen before any measurement):** the campaign closes on the first of — (a) primary goal achieved; (b) characterization NO-GO on every authorized grammar branch; (c) 4 step-2 contacts spent without a pass; (d) 2 slates + their pre-registered contingency chains exhausted; (e) Lei stops it. Closure returns to PAUSE-AND-HARVEST with a 7-R-class retrospective regardless of outcome.

## §2. The core approach — the Machine–Alpha–Universe triangle

The triangle is operated as a **design-time co-evolution loop in the free zone, converging before anything is spent**:

```
        MACHINE (fixed constants + additive extensions)
          ▲  constrains both, extended only additively
          │
   ┌──────┴──────┐
   ▼             ▼
UNIVERSE ◄────► ALPHA (hypothesis + mutation)
 screens shaped   grammar demands shape
 by grammar needs  the universe screens
```

- **Machine vertex:** constants are law (fee = 43.75/price bps at clip scale; floors 4.7–6.2 stressed; boundaries 78/25/12 per session; warm bands 1–5 quotes/s; conjunctive IC ⇒ required RankIC ≈ 2.576/√(n−3)). The only permitted machine moves are the pack-15 §8 *additive* extensions (cross-sectional IC path; spread-state DV path); core changes stay backlogged.
- **Universe vertex:** derived, not assumed — the pack-15 §4 spec sheet instantiated with cutoffs frozen by the brain before the implementor measures (the two-agent split enforces the characterization discipline structurally). Rolling selection, if used, freezes the *function* per §6 of the doctrine.
- **Alpha vertex — hypothesis + mutation, with the ledger made safe for mutation:** mutation is a **design-time operator only**. Mutants are generated, registered drafted-not-evaluated (N-impact 0), and killed for free by the E∧P∧M projection and gates G-A…G-H; at most one survivor per lineage reaches a census; one lineage per slate reaches step 2 unless a frozen contingency chain says otherwise. Any mutant evaluated against outcomes = +1 N, authorized in advance, no exceptions. Mutation without this rule is p-hacking with a genetics vocabulary; with it, it is cheap-zone search — which is exactly where round 1 proved search belongs (~64% of threads died free).
- **Convergence criterion for one loop iteration:** a (grammar branch × universe cohort × mechanism lineage) cell where E∧P∧M projects satisfiable **on measured inputs with the calibration-record margins** (unmeasured arms budgeted ≤ 0.5×; density ≥ 130 design-central; required IC at achievable n ≤ the branch's defended plausible IC). Only a converged cell may spend a census.
